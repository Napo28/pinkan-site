Team required:

This is the minimum stat for Speed and ATK only. If I put 31 bulk, you don't need it, but the more you have, the better.

Only check IV and EV, except for Annihilape where you can check the total stats.

Pre-toxic your Floatzel with Toxic Orb.

Items needed:

Need: Firium Z, Waterium Z, Specs, Band, Scarf, Charcoal, Leftovers, Eviolite

Better need: Fightium Z, Groundium Z, Rocky Helmet

How to play:

Always put Chansey on 6th slot unless you need it.

You will need trees inside Ice room and Sleep room.
If you don't know if you can activate it fast, just play 2:30 song, check what room it is with the clouds (from left to right: Ice, Fire, Poison, Sleep).
Then it will be either Ice or Sleep but never both together.

Take the ladder, go to the center, take the ladder again, go up, cross the bridge, go up again until you find a door and go inside it.
If the clouds say Ice, go northwest. If it's Sleep, go northeast until you find a tree, go inside, press the lever and take the hole to go back to the piano.

Once you’ve done 2 rooms, go back into Ice or Sleep room, go north and take the door, go north again, wait until the song is over and press the lever to switch rooms.

Rooms part (5 mins song)

Ice Room

Go to the left door in piano room.
Put Magcargo in 3rd slot to avoid the curse.
Go south to find a lever to unlock the boss room, then back to the entrance, go west to the boss.

Honchkrow: Cloyster Icicle Spear ×3
Vivillon: Cloyster Scarf Icicle Spear ×2
Cacturne: Annihilape Rocky Helmet Drain Punch

Swellow: Cloyster Leftovers Shell Smash (if Tailwind → Shell Smash again, else Iron Defense → Shell Smash → Icicle Spear ×2)

Fire Room

Take the ladder, go down and left, follow Lapras to reach the Fire room door.
Put Floatzel on 4th slot and Magcargo on 5th slot.
Kill Accelgor on the path with Blacephalon Flame Charge ×2 then Flamethrower ×2.

Pyroar: Floatzel Choice Band Low Kick ×3
Exploud: Blacephalon Z Flamethrower → Flamethrower ×2
Accelgor: Blacephalon Leftovers Flame Charge ×2 → Flamethrower ×2

Volcarona: Blacephalon Firium Z → Flame Charge → Sub → Flame Charge → Z Flamethrower → Flamethrower

Poison Room

Take the ladder, go right, follow the rightmost Crobat to reach the Poison room door.
Kill Politoed and Ludicolo with Chansey stall.
Lilligant: Cloyster Scarf Icicle Spear ×2 → then proceed to boss.

Ludicolo: Blacephalon Z Flamethrower → Flamethrower
Bellossom: Cloyster Scarf Icicle Spear ×3
Venomoth: Blacephalon Flamethrower

Roserade: Cloyster Leftovers Shell Smash → Icicle Spear ×2

Sleep Room

Go to right door in piano room.
Put Annihilape on 3rd slot.
Go north → east → south → west to find lever to unlock the boss room.
Kill Audino with Blacephalon Z Flamethrower → Shadow Ball ×2.
Go back to entrence then go south cross the bridge and go boss

Chimecho: Blacephalon Specs Shadow Ball ×2
Kricketune: Blacephalon Specs Shadow Ball ×2

Pinkan Form

Heal Blacephalon and use Max Ether (talk to NPC at dungeon entrance).
Press piano for 5 min song, take ladder, go up, cross bridge, go up again and kill 5 pink Pokemon (top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right, center).

Nidoking: Floatzel Z Liquidation
Donphan: Cloyster Choice Band Icicle Spear
Rhyperior: Floatzel Z Liquidation
Whiscash: Annihilape Band Bullet Seed
Klefki: Floatzel Z Dig

Once you’ve killed all, a new room appears. Go north until boss room.

Boss Room

For bot-left: put Floatzel 3rd slot. For top-right: put Annihilape 3rd slot.
Other Klefkis: Blacephalon Specs Flamethrower.
